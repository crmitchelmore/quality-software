# Vision: Quality Software

Quality Software helps teams preserve codebase coherence while delegating larger scopes of work to AI agents.

AI code often fails by drifting from project intent: duplicating abstractions, bypassing canonical helpers, eroding boundaries, and introducing inconsistent conventions faster than humans can restate the architecture.

The strategy is to turn each repository's chosen philosophies, patterns, and canonical implementations into a reviewable profile backed by a knowledge catalogue and a runtime-agnostic conformance engine.

The engine should guide agents at write time, support reviewers at PR time, and map whole-codebase evidence for brownfield adoption, while keeping most findings advisory and reserving blocking for deterministic, certified checks.

The first proof is narrow and measurable: fewer duplicate implementations and architecture-drift findings in AI-authored pull requests without adding noisy gates that teams disable.
